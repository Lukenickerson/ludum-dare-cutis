8 hours ago | November 22nd, 2014 12:41 pm

I mentioned I’d participate in Compo earlier, but I had a change of heart, recently. As one of the organizers at Tech Valley Game Space (a physical location in Troy, NY), a ton of time will be taken to help other developers to start their game projects. Since that takes time, and I want to help those participating in the game jam, I’ll be switching to the Jam competition.

Here are tentatively a few presentations we had in mind:

  • “How to survive Ludum Dare”
  • “Make it Juicy: easy methods to make your game more engaging”
  • “Getting votes: the Ludum Dare marketing guide.”
